MEET OUR COACHES

Raymond Koh

Head Coach

Raymond Koh is the founder and head coach of MAS Badminton Club. He has over 15 years of professional coaching experience in Malaysia and Canada, producing a tremendous amount of award-winning students at provincial and national tournaments, as well as the international stage.

He has also coached Team Canada at the 2019 PAN AMERICAN Junior Championships in Moncton and the 2023 PAN AMERICAN Junior Championships in Lima, showcasing his expertise and dedication to the sport.

Raymond's passion for badminton and commitment to helping players reach their full potential make him an exceptional leader for our coaching staff.

Adwin Lau

High-Performance Coach

Adwin Lau is one of our high-performance coaches at MAS Badminton. With over 10 years of coaching experience in Canada, he's coached many top junior provincial, national, and international players.

During his competition years, he was a two-time national champion and competed in international tournaments including the Junior Pan American Championships and the 2018 World University Championships in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Adwin's persistence and commitment to reaching excellence for all players make him a phenomenal high-performance coach.

Damian Cheung

High-Performance Coach

Damian Cheung is one of our high-performance coaches at MAS Badminton. He has over 12 years of coaching experience in Canada and many years of competition experience in Hong Kong before becoming a coach. He has also coached many top junior provincial, national, and international players.

During his competition years, he was part of the Junior National Hong Kong Team, winning numerous provincial and national champion titles.

Damian's passion and coaching expertise has always helped his students reach their goals and perform well in competition.
