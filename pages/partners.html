PARTNERS

J Zone Badminton

JZone Badminton is your one-stop destination for all things badminton, tennis, and pickleball. Specializing in high-quality equipment, apparel, and accessories, JZone caters to players of all levels, ensuring they have everything they need to excel on the court. Additionally, JZone offers professional racket stringing services to help athletes maintain peak performance. With a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, JZone is a trusted hub for racquet sports enthusiasts, providing expert guidance, top-tier products, and services to elevate your game.

Toronto Metropolitan University Badminton Club

Toronto Metropolitan University Badminton Club stands as a vital pillar in our badminton community through their unwavering dedication to promoting the sport. Their commitment extends beyond the court - through regular fundraising events, they provide crucial financial support that helps Canadian badminton athletes cover competition fees and other expenses. As a sponsor, MAS Badminton recognizes their shared vision and values in advancing Canadian badminton, making them a trusted partner in developing the sport's future.

Ajax Pickering Minor Hockey

The Ajax Pickering Minor Hockey Association (APMHA) creates an environment where players of all ages and abilities can thrive in the sport they love. Their commitment to fostering a safe, enjoyable, and supportive atmosphere allows young athletes to develop both their hockey skills and strong character through mutual respect and teamwork. As a proud sponsor, we align with their mission to encourage youth participation in sports and promote active, healthy lifestyles in our community.

AchieveMore Education

AchieveMore Education (AME) Established in 2010, AME is an Academic and Arts enrichment learning and counseling Organization for SK to Grade 12 in Toronto, Canada. They offer programs by on-site and online on mathematics, science (physics, chemistry, biology, computer science, etc), language (English, French, Chinese & Japanese), fine arts, school application and exam preparation, learning skills and many more!
