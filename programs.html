PROGRAMS

Private Lessons

Focus personalized coaching for players of all levels

$85-$120 + HST/hour

  • Learn proper skills, technique, and footwork
  • Tailored and detailed feedback
  • 1v1 and 1v2 instruction
  • Flexible scheduling

Recreational Team

Group classes for beginner and amateur players

$28-$30 + HST/class

  • Develop fundamental skills and techniques
  • Learn in a fun, group environment
  • Professional coaching
  • Beginner/intermediate drills

Competition Team

High-intensity team training for competition players

$30-$35 + HST/class

  • Intensive drills and conditioning
  • Tournament preparation
  • Advanced strategy development
  • Five different competition teams determined by skill

Players interested in joining a team higher than beginner group must complete a 5-minute skill assessment with a coach, who will evaluate your abilities to find a suitable team for your level.
