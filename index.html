OUR SERVICES

Private Lessons

Personalized coaching for players of all levels

  • tailored and detailed feedback to improve performance

Recreational Training

Recreational group classes for beginner and amateur players

  • develop fundamental skills and techniques
  • develop interest in badminton, balancing learning and fun

Competition Training

High-intensity team training for competition players

  • intensive drills, match play, and physical conditioning
  • prepare for provincial, national, and international tournaments
